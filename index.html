Make It Yours

Custom options for every piece.

Every table, bench, and custom build starts with your choices. Pick the stain, the pattern, and the leg style — or describe something else and J.T. will make it work.

Stain & Paint

Choose from 12 wood stains — golden oak to dark walnut — or 8 paint colors. Samples available so you know exactly what you're getting before you order.

Straight planks, diagonal, chevron, or herringbone — the pattern becomes the centerpiece. Pick the one that fits your style, or ask J.T. about something custom.

About J.T.

Built with Alabama pride since 2017.

J.T. Taylor has been building custom furniture by hand from his Alabama shop since 2017. Every piece starts with a conversation — J.T. takes the time to understand exactly what you need, then builds it from scratch, the way it should be built.

No shortcuts. No flat-pack. No mass production. Just solid wood, real craftsmanship, and a builder who takes pride in every joint, every finish, and every delivery.

J.T. delivers every piece personally and sets it up in your home himself — because for him, the job isn't done until you're happy with it.
